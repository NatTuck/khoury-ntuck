## Shell Operators

 - Redirect input: sort < foo.txt
 - Redirect output: sort foo.txt > output.txt
 - Pipe: sort foo.txt | uniq
 - Background: sleep 10 &
 - And: true && echo one
 - Or: true || echo one
 - Semicolon: echo one; echo two
